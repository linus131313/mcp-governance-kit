# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful. Assume good faith.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Disagreements about technical substance are welcome; disagreements
  about people are not.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to **hey@linus-teklenburg.de**. Reports are
handled confidentially and in good faith.
